Description

This Magento module allows you to improve the usability of your Magento 2 store by enriching product attributes and its options as well as product options with descriptive tooltips/hints that work beautifully in every browser on any device. You can create hints for different store views.

Key Features:

  • Upload Shipping Rates in Bulk using CSV
  • Offer Various Combinations of Shipping Rates based
  • This Magento module allows you to improve the usability of your Magento 2 store by enriching product attributes and its options as well as product options with descriptive tooltips/hints that work beautifully in every browser on any device. You can create hints for different store views.
